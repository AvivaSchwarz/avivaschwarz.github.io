About

I am a freshman at the Bronx High School of Schience in New York City, and have become a member of the all-girls robotics team, FRC team 2265, a participant in the mathematics club, and a member of the Bronx Science Hackers. When I'm not in school, I work on my software to help people communicate in natural disasters, or my various scientific research projects related to data analysis and climate mitigation. My hobbies range from programming, doing kenkens, playing the cello, and reading.

I am always looking for opportunities to talk to young girls about their potential in STEM or discuss changing the workplace one woman at a time with companies. Feel free to contact me at aviva.schwarz@gmail.com to learn more!

Education

The Bronx High School of Science 2017- 2021

The Bronx High School of Science (commonly called Bronx Science or Science, and formerly Science High) is an elite public high school in New York City. It is one of nine "specialized" public high schools located in New York City and operated by the New York City Department of Education. Bronx Science is considered one of the most prestigious and selective high schools in the United States.

Projects

Student Overflow

My first hackathon project, Student Overflow, is an application designed by students, for students. Student Overflow optimizes the forum- based question and answer uses of yahoo answers and reddit, but without the unecessary mess. We used Material Design principles to create an intuitive user interface, and we provided the features that we know students search for when they look for academic help online.
